About Me

Hi, I'm Kensukeken!
You might know me as Ken, Kenzy, Akira, or Kokichi, depending on the server—typically on Discord. None of these are my real name unless we've met in person. My pronouns are she/her. I'm a painter artist, a programmer, and a lifelong learner. Welcome to my personal website, where I showcase my work and share my thoughts.

My love for creativity and technology drives everything I do. Fluent in Turkmen, Arabic, and Turkish, I bring a unique cultural perspective to my work.

My Hobbies

I really like Discord-it's basically the favorite place to connect and chill in. If I am not coding or working on my art, you can usually find me in the background playing Geometry Dash, osu! catch, chess, Roblox, Heart of Tibet, Giza, or Danganronpa V3. I've played Geometry Dash since 2015, and it's still one of my favorite games. If you're curious about more of my gaming adventures, feel free to check out my Steam profile linked below.

Outside gaming, I enjoy tinkering with projects and learning new things. Delving into technology-based ideas and learning new skills is very enjoyable. Mathematics, art, and especially LaTeXLa-TEX are quite interesting to me. LaTeX helps me format my works; it's clean and professional. I wrote my first paper on mathematical analysis of a triple integral using gamma function and binomial series, formatted in LaTeX. I code, create art, or level up to achieve something meaningful which I share with others.

I enjoy reading books, listening to music. I like to watch movies and Kdrama in my free time. I enjoy watching mystery, thriller, and horror movies. I like to read mystery, thriller, and horror books. I also enjoy reading about mathematics and computer science. I listen to a variety of music genres, including pop, rock, and classical.
