Documents

Explore my collection of PDFs, including research papers, tutorials, and guides that I've created or found helpful in my journey. Feel free to download and read through them!

  • ICS3U-FSE-Integral-Area-Research-Paper

    This research paper delves into the process of calculating the area under a curve using integrals, complemented by code snippets in C++ to illustrate the practical application of these concepts. It provides a comprehensive analysis and detailed examples to support understanding.

  • MHF4U-FSE-Final-Summative-Evaluation

    This document contains my final summative evaluation for the Advanced Functions course (MHF4U). It includes detailed solutions and analysis of key mathematical concepts such as asymptotes, trigonometric identities, logarithms, and function transformations. It's a comprehensive review of the skills and knowledge gained throughout the course, presented in a 34-page report.

  • MCR3U-Functions-University-Notes

    These notes are designed for the MCR3U Functions course, covering essential topics such as polynomial, exponential, and logarithmic functions. They aim to provide a solid foundation in these concepts, which are crucial for advanced mathematics studies at the university level.

  • MHF4U-Advanced-Functions-Notes

    This document includes detailed notes on advanced functions, focusing on trigonometric, polynomial, and rational functions. It serves as a key resource for students taking the MHF4U course, with clear explanations and illustrative examples to aid in mastering complex mathematical concepts.

  • MCV4U-Calculus-and-Vectors-Notes

    These notes offer an in-depth exploration of calculus and vectors, including topics such as derivatives, integrals, and vector operations. They are designed to support students in the MCV4U course with thorough explanations and practical examples to enhance their learning experience.

  • OLC4O-Hidden-Figures

    Hidden Figures is a powerful film that tells the story of three African-American women who worked at Langley Aeronautical Laboratory in Hampton, Virginia, during the early 1940s. These remarkable women played crucial roles in advancing American aviation and space technology, contributing significantly to NASA's success during the Space Race.
