Should grade 12 University level English be a requirement for entry into all university programs?

I would argue it should, and here are my reasons why:
The Key Elements of Communication: English is essential for effective communication. Strong English abilities are required for explaining mathematical concepts, which makes business talks, and writing reports. Grade 12 English helps students think carefully and communicate properly.
Building Critical Thinking: In Grade 12 English includes more than just reading and writing. It is about seriously studying literature, collecting additional points of view, and giving powerful proof. These skills are necessary for university success.
Learning across subjects: English courses usually include history, philosophy, and social studies, giving a general education. This teaches students to think formally and make connections throughout complex courses.
Getting Ready for Jobs: Good communication skills are essential in any occupation. Grade 12 English makes sure that students have improved these abilities, offering them a benefit upon the job market.
Conclusion: In conclusion, every university programs should require Grade 12 English. It allows effective communication, increases critical thinking, creates together learning, and ready students for career.
