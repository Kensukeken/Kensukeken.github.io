Story Summary

The Hidden Figures is a story about an African woman, who work at Langley Aeronautical Laboratory in Hampton, Virginia, starting in the early 1940s. The women play an integral role in the development of American aviation and space technology. If you like stories about space or people who work hard to do amazing things, you’ll like this movie. It is also good for people who like to learn about the past. I give Hidden Figures 5 stars out of 5 because it is a great story. It makes you feel good and teaches you something new. The movie shows that anyone can do big things if they try hard enough. It is essential to remember and celebrate everyone who does something special, even if others did not notice them before.

Lesson or Moral

The central lesson of Hidden Figures is that determination and hard work can lead to extraordinary achievements, regardless of the obstacles. The film underscores the importance of recognizing and celebrating the contributions of individuals who might otherwise go unnoticed, highlighting the value of diversity and inclusion in achieving great things.

Review

I highly recommend the movie Hidden Figures to my friends. It is a movie that shows the story of couple amazing African American women who worked at NASA during the race to space. These women did great things that not many people know about. The movie is exciting like a superhero story, but it is all true. It also reveals to us how necessary it is to work together and respect everyone. Because of this movie, more people, especially girls and those from different backgrounds, want to follow their dreams in science, Engineering and mathematics (STEM).

To start with, In the movie Hidden Figures can teach you a history of space race. For instance, the movie shows how African American women made serious contributions to NASA during the Space Race. You will discover something not many people talk about. This establishes the significance of the movie to help you learn more about the future once you get a job at NASA and help others.

In addition, Hidden Figures celebrates getting inspired and teamwork. The movie is all about empowerment. It shows how these women faced challenges and difficulty times but still succeeded. It is like a superhero story, but real. It demonstrates that progress happens faster when people with different backgrounds work together. While this point might not be as strong, it still highlights the value of including everyone.

In conclusion, I would recommend the movie Hidden Figures to my friends. It is a movie that helps you learn about the history of the space race, the crucial work that African American women made at NASA, and the history of the movie itself. In other words, it is exciting to see how they get better of challenges and succeed against all difficulties. This movie is not just enjoyable; it supports us to believe that everyone can make a difference in the world, no matter who they are or where they come from. Watching Hidden Figures can motivate us to learn more, do more, and be more caught in everything we do.
